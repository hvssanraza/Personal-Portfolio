About Me

I'm a passionate Artificial Intelligence student with a strong foundation in software development and a creative background in design. My journey in technology combines technical expertise with artistic sensibility, allowing me to build solutions that are both powerful and user-friendly.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor's in AI, I've spent the last several years honing my skills in Python, C++, and web technologies while applying them to real-world projects. My background includes:

  • 4 years in music production and sound engineering

What excites me most is the intersection of artificial intelligence and human-centered design. I believe the most impactful technologies are those that combine cutting-edge algorithms with intuitive interfaces and thoughtful user experiences.

When I'm not coding, you can find me composing music, designing visuals, or exploring the latest advancements in machine learning. I'm always open to collaborating on interesting projects or discussing new ideas at the intersection of technology and creativity.
